Frequently Asked Questions

You can create an account by clicking the "Register" button on the top right corner of the page. You will be asked to provide a username, email, and password.

To apply for a job, you must be logged in. Navigate to the job details page and click the "Apply Now" button. You will be asked to provide a message and upload your CV.

To post a job, you must have an employer account. From the employer dashboard, click the "Post a New Job" button and fill out the required information.
